Dear Principal / CS Department Head,

We're reaching out because we believe every student deserves access to a real computer — one they can actually use to learn programming, run a local server, or explore an operating system without hitting a wall of restrictions.

OpenKeys is a nonprofit that grants fully functional, unrestricted computers to K-12 schools at zero cost. Schools take permanent ownership — we impose no MDM software, no content filters, no lock-down. Just tools that work.

"To bridge the digital divide in K-12 education by providing unrestricted computers to schools — empowering students with real-world computing access."

— OpenKeys Mission Statement

What your school receives

💻

Fully unlocked computers

Linux-ready machines capable of running Python, VS Code, and real development tools.

🔓

Zero MDM or filters

We never impose remote management or content restrictions. Your school controls the devices.

📋

Permanent ownership

Devices are granted — not loaned. Your school owns them outright with no buyback clauses.

📅

Fast 60–90 day delivery

From application to computers in your classroom — typically within one semester.

Who qualifies

✓

Title I or under-resourced schools — we prioritize schools with documented financial need.
✓

Active CS or coding program — or a documented plan to launch one with access to proper hardware.
✓

Commitment to unrestricted use — devices must be used for CS instruction. No resale. One annual report (under 15 minutes to complete).
✓

K–12, any grade band — elementary coding clubs to high school AP CS are all eligible.

Applying takes under 10 minutes. We'll follow up within one week to confirm eligibility and discuss logistics. First batches of 10–30 computers ship within 60–90 days of agreement signing.
